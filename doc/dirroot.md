# Dir root objects

Dir root objects have the following methods associated with them:
- `dir root.chdir()`
- `dir root.chmod(path, mode)`
- `dir root.chown(path, uid, gid)`
- `dir root.close()`
- `dir root.create(name)`
- `dir root.createBin(name)`
- `dir root.isClosed()`
- `dir root.lchown(path, uid, gid)`
- `dir root.link(target, linkName)`
- `dir root.mkdir(name)`
- `dir root.mkdirp(name)`
- `dir root.name()`
- `dir root.open(name, mode)`
- `dir root.readFile(name)`
- `dir root.readFileBin(name)`
- `dir root.readLink(name)`
- `dir root.remove(path)`
- `dir root.removeAll(path)`
- `dir root.rename(oldPath, newPath)`
- `dir root.rmrf(path)`
- `dir root.setChain(chainBool)`
- `dir root.stat(path)`
- `dir root.symlink(target, linkName)`
- `dir root.writeFile(path, string)`
